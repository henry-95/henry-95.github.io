My Projects

Check out all this fancy stuff!

YardandGroom What I am currently working on. I update and maintain the website. I fix bugs, implement new features, manage social media and answer customer enquiries.
Boxing Trainer A hobby project. I had the idea of creating a customisable boxing trainer that would announce out loud each move. The 'player' can then customise how many rounds they want, what moves they want to perform, e.g punches, knees, elbows, kicks.
Chess Board Coordinates Teacher A hobby project. I like to play chess but wanted to learn the ranks/files of the board. I wanted to create a tool where you can learn the rank or file separately (e.g. D, or 4) rather than at the same time (e.g. D4). Haven't started this yet!
