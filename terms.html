1. Services Provided

The Provider agrees to design, host, and maintain a professional website for the Client using a concierge subscription model, including:

  • Hosting & Security: Provision of high-speed hosting, SSL certificate, and daily backups.
  • Technical Maintenance: Regular updates to site software and plugins to ensure security.
  • Monthly Updates: Up to one (1) hour of content updates per calendar month (e.g., text, images, or horse listings).

2. Fees and Payment Terms

  • Initial Setup Fee: A one-time, non-refundable fee of £100 is due upon signing.
  • Monthly Subscription: A fee of £75 per month is payable in advance via Stripe.
  • Exceeding Inclusive Time: Work beyond the included 1 hour per month is billed at £25 per hour, subject to Client approval.
  • Non-Payment: If payment is not received within 7 days of the due date, the Provider reserves the right to suspend the website until the balance is paid.

3. 6-Month Minimum Term

To account for the subsidized setup and design costs, this agreement is subject to a minimum term of 6 months.

  • Should the Client cancel before the 6-month term expires, the remaining balance for the period becomes due immediately.
  • After 6 months, the contract moves to a rolling monthly basis.

4. Communication & Support

  • Support Channel: Direct Phone/WhatsApp or email access is provided for support.
  • Standard Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
  • Turnaround: Standard updates are typically completed within 48–72 hours.

5. Ownership & Cancellation

  • Ownership: Upon completion of the 6-month term, the Client owns the website design and provided content.
  • Notice Period: Following the minimum term, the Client may cancel with 30 days’ written notice.
  • Transfer: If the Client cancels, the Provider will provide a backup of site files. A migration fee of £150 applies if the Provider is required to assist in moving the site to a new host.
